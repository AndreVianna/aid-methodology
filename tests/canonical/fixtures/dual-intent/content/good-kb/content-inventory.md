# Content Inventory

## Article publishing

Allows authors to draft, review, and publish long-form written content. Articles
are indexed for search and linked from the /blog and /learn sections.

## Tutorial authoring

Allows subject-matter experts to create step-by-step instructional content with
difficulty ratings. Tutorials support prerequisite chaining and are linked from
the /learn section.

## Content validation

Validates all content at submission time against the content-model contracts.
Validation rules are type-specific (Article vs Tutorial) and enforce slug patterns,
field types, and publishing-state transitions.

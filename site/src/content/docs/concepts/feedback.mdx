Found a problem or have something to share? Pick the type below and fill in the form. There's no
backend and no tracking — the form builds a **prefilled GitHub issue and opens it in a new tab**,
where you review and submit it under your own GitHub account. Nothing is sent until you click
*Submit new issue* on GitHub.

<FeedbackForms />

## What happens after you submit

Your issue lands in the [AID repository](https://github.com/AndreVianna/aid-methodology/issues)
with the right label applied automatically — `site issue`, `bug`, or `feedback`. Maintainers triage
it there and address it in a future release. Because you submit under your own account, you'll get
GitHub notifications on any replies.

You can also browse [existing issues](https://github.com/AndreVianna/aid-methodology/issues) first —
your problem may already be reported.

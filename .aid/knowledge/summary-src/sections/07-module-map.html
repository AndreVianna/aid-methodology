Module Map

AID's major modules across four planes: Distribution (install and CLI), Toolkit (skills, agents, scripts), Render (profile generator), and Observation (dashboard). Understanding the planes is prerequisite to any module-touching change.

AID's four planes: Distribution (install, CLI, packages), Toolkit (skills, agents, scripts, templates), Render (profile generator + VERIFY gate), Observation (dashboard server, reader, kb.html). The Toolkit plane (teal border) is the most frequently changed.
